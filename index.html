ATAH – Intimacy with God

The Ministry is a God ordained movement birthed with one core mandate: to draw people into deep, personal, and unbroken communion with the Father.

The name ATAH, meaning Intimacy with God, reflects our conviction that true transformation flows from closeness with Jesus — not religion, not routine, but relationship.

What God Has Given Us

Our Divine Assignment

God has entrusted us with a sacred calling — to carry His presence, demonstrate His love, and release His truth into every place we are sent. This is not merely a programme or activity; it is a stewardship of spiritual life and power that transforms hearts and renews communities. He has given us the gift of His Spirit to lead, comfort and empower us, granting wisdom for decisions, boldness for proclamation, and compassion for the broken. Through the Holy Spirit we are equipped to worship in spirit and in truth and to minister with grace.

To create platforms for a lifestyle of worship.

Our mission is to establish spaces where worship is not an event but a daily walk — empowering believers to live in constant fellowship with the Holy Spirit.

We seek to nurture intimacy, spiritual growth, and revival through Spirit-led encounters that transform hearts and realign lives to God’s purpose.

To raise an end-time army driven by intimacy and obedience.

Our vision is to build a generation that passionately pursues God, carries His presence everywhere they go, and transforms nations through unwavering devotion and spiritual authority.

We envision believers who ignite revival, influence culture, and walk boldly in God’s purpose for their lives.
